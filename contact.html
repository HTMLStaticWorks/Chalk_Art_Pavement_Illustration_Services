Before You Book

Please have the following information ready to help us provide an accurate quote.

Dimensions

What is the approximate size of the area you want to be illustrated?

Duration

How long do you need the art to last? (Standard chalk vs. semi-permanent).

What is the date of your event, and when would artists have access to the site?
