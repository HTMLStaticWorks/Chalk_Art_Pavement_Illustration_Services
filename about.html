Our Story

Bringing temporary art to permanent memories.

We are a Collective of Visionaries

Founded in 2015, ChalkMaster began as a small group of street artists who wanted to change how people interact with urban spaces. Today, we are a global agency representing the finest pavement illustrators.

Our mission is simple: to transform the mundane grey of the city into a vibrant canvas for imagination and storytelling.

Our Journey

How we grew from a small collective to a global agency.

2015

Founded by Julian Vester in a small studio in Berlin with 3 fellow artists.

2018

Created our first viral 3D mural for the International Street Art Festival.

2021

Expanded to representation in 15 countries and 25 full-time artists.

2024

Awarded "Innovative Design Agency of the Year" for our urban activation projects.
